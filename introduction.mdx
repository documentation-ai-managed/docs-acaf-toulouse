## Overview

Acaf-toulouse provides a centralized space for your teams to create, organize, and collaborate on project documentation. You can build structured knowledge bases, wikis, and guides without the complexity of traditional tools. Focus on content while the platform handles versioning, search, and access controls.

This documentation space serves as your hub. Use it to document APIs, user guides, internal processes, or onboarding materials efficiently.

## Benefits for Teams and Projects

You streamline onboarding by centralizing all project knowledge in one place. Teams reduce time spent searching for information, minimizing errors and boosting productivity. Version control ensures compliance and audit trails for regulated industries.

Projects benefit from consistent documentation standards. Integrate with tools like GitHub for seamless workflows, turning scattered notes into professional guides.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Sign up at `https://app.acaf-toulouse.com/register` with your email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Space" icon="folder-plus" title-type="p">
    From the dashboard, click "New Space" and name it for your project.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add First Document" icon="file-plus" title-type="p">
    Inside your space, create a new page. Use the Markdown editor to add content.

    <CodeGroup show-lines="true" tabs={[]}>
      ```markdown
      # Welcome to My Project

      This is your first document.

      ## Quick Links
      - [API Docs](/api)
      - [Setup Guide](/setup)
      ```


  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite Team" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    Share the space link or add members via email for collaboration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
