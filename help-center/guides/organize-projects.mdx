## Overview

Keep your documentation organized by grouping related pages into projects.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Click "New Project" on your dashboard and enter a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documentation pages">
    Select your project, click "Add Page," and fill in the details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange pages">
    Drag and drop pages to reorder them in your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
