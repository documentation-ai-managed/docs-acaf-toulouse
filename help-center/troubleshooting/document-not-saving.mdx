---
title: Document Not Saving
description: Fix document save issues in Acaf-toulouse.
---

## Symptoms

- Edits are not saved
- "Could not save document" message

## Causes

- Lost internet connection
- Session expired

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    ````bash
    ping acaf-toulouse.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh your session">
    Log out and log back in to renew your access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
